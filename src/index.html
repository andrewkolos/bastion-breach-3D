Bastion Breach

Made by Andrew Kolos

You and the computer will play a round for each card in the middle, starting with the leftmost card.

Click on a card in your hand to play it against the middle card and the card the computer plays.

The highest ranked card of the three wins the round, getting the winner a point.

The ace is specialâ€”it beats any face card but loses to any number.

If neither of the player cards beats the other two cards, then the round ends in a stalemate. However, the next round will be worth an additional point (these can add up). For example, if there are three stalemates in a row, the next round is worth 4 points.

Player wins are denoted with green circles, computer wins with red crosses, and stalemates with blue squares.
